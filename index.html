How It works

DIRECTORY OF CAKE SHOPS WORK IN FOUR SIMPLE STEPS

1 - SEARCH:

It's simple, in Directory of Pastrys home page, enter the name of the pastry store you're looking for and click on search. Alternatively, click on the search link on the top menu to view the list of all avaible pastry.

2 - LOCATE:

Get to see where the Cake Shops is located by clicking on the cake shop profile. It's even more exciting. You can get the directions from anywhere in the country.

3 - CONTACT:

Contact the Cake Shop of your choice by clicking on the phone icon next to his name in the profile page. You can ask the menu and add some additional items.

4 - ORDER:

Once you choose the cake you want, you can arrange the payment with the seller. Then just wait until is deliverid in your and enjoy. Bon Apetit!
